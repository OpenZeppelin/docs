## OpenZeppelin plugins

Plugins maintained by OpenZeppelin.

* [openzeppelin-nile-upgrades](https://github.com/OpenZeppelin/openzeppelin-nile-upgrades)

  Deploy and manage upgradeable contracts on StarkNet.

## Community plugins

Community maintained plugins.

* [nile-coverage](https://github.com/ericnordelo/nile-coverage)

  Add coverage reports for Cairo Smart Contracts (from pytest test suite).

* [nile-verifier-plugin](https://github.com/martriay/nile-verifier-plugin)

  Verify contracts on [starkscan.co](https://starkscan.co/).
