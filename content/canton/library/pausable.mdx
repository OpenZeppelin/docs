Pausable is an emergency stop mechanism: an authorized party can halt sensitive choices, and resume them once the situation is resolved.

## Overview

Pausable exposes a paused state and the ability to flip it. Workflows guard their sensitive choices so that they fail closed while paused. This gives operators a safe, reversible way to respond to incidents without tearing down or migrating a workflow.

## When to use it

Add Pausable to any workflow that may need a controlled halt: for example a token that should stop transfers during an incident, or a settlement flow that should stop accepting new legs while an issue is investigated. Pair it with [Access Control](/canton/library/access-control) or [Ownable](/canton/library/ownable) to decide who is allowed to pause and resume.
